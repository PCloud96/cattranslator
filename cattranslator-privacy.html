Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

Cloud Motion Lab LLC built Cat Translator: Meow & Purr as a commercial application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information.

Information Collection

Cat Translator does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not require account creation, and we do not track your identity.

Microphone Access

The app requests microphone access solely to record cat sounds for translation. Audio is processed entirely on your device and is never uploaded to any server. Recordings are temporary and discarded after translation.

Camera Access

The Cat Cam feature uses your device camera to record video with live translation overlays. Videos are saved locally to your device and are never uploaded or shared by us.

Advertising

Cat Translator may display ads through Unity Ads. Unity Ads may collect device identifiers and usage data as described in their privacy policy. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Purchase records are stored locally on your device.

Local Data Storage

App preferences and settings are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This data is not transmitted to any external server.

Children's Privacy

Cat Translator is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Third-Party Services

The app may use the following third-party services:

Unity Ads — for displaying advertisements
RevenueCat — for managing subscriptions and purchases
TelemetryDeck — for privacy-first, anonymous usage analytics

These services may collect anonymous usage data. None of these services collect personally identifiable information through our app.

Entertainment Disclaimer

Cat Translator generates translations using AI for entertainment purposes only. Translations do not represent actual cat communication and should not be used for veterinary or behavioral assessment.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
